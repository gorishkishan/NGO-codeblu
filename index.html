OUR STORY

Born out of burnout.
Built to heal.

CodeBlu was born out of burnout — a feeling every healthcare professional knows all too well. We started small, checking in on friends and colleagues, but quickly realised individual effort wasn't enough. In December 2020, we turned that passion into purpose.

Since then, we've run workshops, published research, authored books, and partnered with national organisations — all with one goal: to make sure no healer feels alone in their struggle. In 2026, CodeBlu became a registered NGO, marking the start of what we believe will be a truly national movement.

  1. 2020

    A quiet beginning

    A small circle of healers started checking in on burnt-out colleagues — no funding, no name, just care.

  2. 2021

    First recognition

    Recognised by the Society for Emergency Medicine India for the ED Wellness Workshop at EMCON 2021.

  3. 2023

    National partnerships

    Partnered with Jhpiego to reach thousands of nursing students and faculty across India.

  4. 2026

    Became an NGO

    CodeBlu registered as a nonprofit — turning a movement of volunteers into a lasting institution.

Because before a healer can heal others, they must first heal themselves.

CAREER COACHING

Request a mentoring session

Before filling this out, take a moment to reflect on your career journey so far — where you are, and where you'd like to go. A little clarity here makes the session far more meaningful.

Once submitted, your mentor will confirm a date and time by email. Please join from a quiet space with stable internet, and keep a notepad handy — there's usually something worth writing down.

Plans changed? Let your mentor know in advance via WhatsApp or email — life happens, a heads-up just helps everyone.
