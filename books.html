RESOURCES

Books & Research from CodeBlu

Everything we've written, published and studied on healthcare professional burnout — put into words so no healer has to feel like the only one going through it.

Our team has authored books and published peer-reviewed research on clinician wellbeing, turning our workshops and conversations into resources the whole profession can use — not just the healers we meet in person.

Book · 2023

Code Blu: Diaries of the Healers

Dr. Amit Nagpal & Dr. Bee

A collection of first-person accounts from doctors and nurses on burnout, breakdown and the slow work of recovery.

Request a copy →
Book · 2024

Rest Is Not a Reward

Dr. Murtuza Ghiya

A short, practical guide to resilience for residents and young doctors, written to be read between shifts.

Request a copy →
Book · 2025

Before You Heal Others

Dr. Vaishnavi Chandramohan

Essays on identity, purpose and burnout among India's healthcare workforce, drawn from CodeBlu's early workshops.

Request a copy →
Free e-book · 2018

The Art of Caring: Emergency Minds

Dr. Murtuza Ghiya, Dr. Kishor M. & Dr. Suhas Chandran

A collaborative concept book with Emergency Medicine Association & the Indian Psychiatric Society, covering burnout, breaking bad news, sleep and the soft skills seldom taught in medical school.
